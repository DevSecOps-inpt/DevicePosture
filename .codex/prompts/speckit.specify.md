# speckit.specify (Local Wrapper)

Use this wrapper to produce high-level architecture/spec artifacts only.

Constraints:
- Keep scope, interfaces, success criteria, and risks at high level.
- Do not track granular execution tasks in the spec.
- Move executable tasks into Beads.

Prompt skeleton:
1. Problem statement and context.
2. Goals, non-goals, and scope boundaries.
3. High-level architecture and major components.
4. Constraints, assumptions, and risks.
5. Acceptance criteria and verification strategy (high level).
